Welcome to Part 1: No Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will make allocation decisions yourself for each of the 10 rounds. Unlike other parts, there is no delegation. You will decide how to allocate the bonuses directly between yourself and the other participant in each round.

How This Works

  1. Decide Your Allocation: In each round, you will write how much of the 100 Ecoins you would like to allocate to the other participant (the Receiver) matched with you in that round.
  2. Reshuffled receivers: You will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  3. You keep the rest:The remainder of the 100 ECoins will be yours to keep.
  4. Once you are satisfied with your decision: Click "Confirm" to finalize your choice for that round. Your decisions are final once submitted and cannot be changed.

Welcome to Part 2: Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will delegate your decisions to an AI agent. Instead of making individual allocation decisions in each round yourself, you will provide input to a supervised learning AI agent by choosing a dataset for your agent will learn from. The agent will use your input to learn your preferences and make allocation decisions on your behalf for all 10 rounds in this part.

How This Works

  1. Train your AI agent: Before the 10 rounds begin, you will choose a table with example allocations that represent your preferences and click “Generate”. This example dataset will be used by your AI agent for it to learn your preferences and generate similar allocation behavior.
  2. Agent Execution: The resulting behavior of your AI agent’s learning will be shown on a table on the right side of the screen. If you do not want your AI agent to proceed with the behavior you see on this table, you have two options:
    • Choose another dataset for your agent to learn based on a set of different preferences.
    • Ask your agent to learn again on the same dataset, and it will generate new behavior on the same preferences.
  3. Reshuffled receivers: Once again, you will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  4. Once you are satisfied with your decision: click "Confirm" to finalize your agent’s settings. The AI agent will use your instructions to allocate the money in all 10 rounds according to the table generated based on the dataset of your choice. Once submitted, you will not be able to adjust the AI agent's decisions once the 10 rounds begin.

Welcome to Part 3: Optional Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will decide whether to delegate all allocation decisions in this part to an AI agent or make all decisions yourself. This choice must be made before any round begins and applies to all 10 rounds in this part. Once you make your decision, it cannot be changed.

How This Works

  1. Decide Upfront: At the start of this part, you will choose whether to:
    • Not delegate and make every allocation decision yourself for all 10 rounds, similarly to what you did in Part 1.
    • Delegate all allocation decisions for all 10 rounds to an AI agent and set up this agent just like you did in Part 2.
  2. Reshuffled receivers: Once again, you will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  3. Irreversible Choice: Your decision applies to all rounds in this part. If you choose to delegate, the AI agent will make all decisions. If you choose not to delegate, you will make every decision yourself.
